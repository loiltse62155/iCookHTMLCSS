Milk Tea

INGREDIENTS 


                Bubble/Boba Milk Tea 

                3 C. water
                4 t. loose leaf black tea or a few tea bags of your favorite tea
                1 C. boba, 5-minute instant type
                1/4 C. turbinado sugar (you can substitute brown or white sugar)
                1/4 C. water
                condensed milk, to your liking
                whole milk, again as much as you like
                ice


INSTRUCTIONS

Bring 4 C. of water to a rapid boil. Add 1. C of uncooked boba to the water. Boil for 5 minutes or until the pearls are chewy and not firm in the middle. Drain and rinse with cold water. Make the simple syrup by dissolving 1/4 C. of sugar to 1/4 C. of boiling water. Place the drained boba in the syrup and allow to soak at least 15 min. The pearls can also be stored in the syrup in the refrigerator for a few days. Reheat them in microwave for 30 seconds (for 1 serving) to soften them up before adding milk & tea.

To assemble add the soaked boba to the bottom of a glass, top with condensed milk, whole milk, ice, and tea. Stir well and adjust flavors to your liking. Make 4-6 servings.
