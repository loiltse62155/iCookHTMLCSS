Oyster Congee (Rice Porridge)

INGREDIENTS 

            75g jasmine rice
            75g glutinous rice (optional, you can use one kind of rice)
            150g oyster
            150g ground pork
            shallot
            ginger
            spring onions
            vietnamese mints (or other herbs)
            roasted onion flakes
            1,5-2l water
