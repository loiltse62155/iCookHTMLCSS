Smoked Blue Fish

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Puree the bluefish, cream cheese, butter, and Cognac in a food processor. Add the onion, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice. Pulse the machine on and off until the ingredients are combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

2.Pack into a serving dish and sprinkle with the nuts, if using.
