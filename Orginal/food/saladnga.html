Russian Salad

INGREDIENTS 

            1 Cup Boiled Peas
            1 Cup Boiled Potato
            1 Cup Boiled Carrots
            1/2 Cup Boiled Corn
            Make Sure all the vegetable are al dente 

            2 Cup Mayonnaise
            1 Tsp Salt 
            1 Tsp Black Pepper
            2 Tbsp Olive Oil
            2 Tsp White Vinagar
