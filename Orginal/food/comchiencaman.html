Fried Rice With Salted Fish

INGREDIENTS 

            3 cups cooked rice (Three Ladies)
            1/2 salted croaker fish
            1/2 lb cooked shrimp (size 16/20)
            2 pieces cooked boneless chicken thighs (diced)
            2 eggs
            1 cup chopped asparagus
            1 small yellow onion (diced)
            1 Tbsp minced ginger
            2 Tbsps minced garlic
            1 tsp chicken powder (Lee Kum Kee)
            1 Tbsp sugar
            1 tsp black pepper
            2 Tbsps vegetable oil
            1/4 cup chopped green onions
            1/4 cup chopped cilantro
