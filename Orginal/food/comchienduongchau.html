Duong Chau Fried Rice

INGREDENTS 

                3 cups 1 day old Rice
                3 Tbsp. Oil
                2 Tbsp. Minced Garlic
                1 Chinese Sausage Stick (Cut into small pieces)
                1/4 cup Dried Shrimp (Soaked in water for at least 30 minutes)
                1 cup Diced Carrots (You can also put peas, Optional)
                2 Eggs
                1 Tbsp. Rice Cooking Wine 
                2 Tbsp. Soy Sauce or to taste. 
                Chopped Green Onions
                A Dash of Black Pepper

DIRECTIONS
In a large pan add 3 Tbsp. Oil and 2 Tbsp. Minced Garlic. Saut√© until slightly brown add in Chinese Sausage.
Stir for a few minutes then add in about 1/4 cup Dried Shrimp. Then add in 3 cups Cooked Rice.
Mix in with the Chinese Sausage and Dried Shrimp. Add in 2 eggs and mix. Mix until the eggs blend in with the rice.
Add in 1 cup Carrots. You can also add in peas too if you would like. Mix it in with the Rice.
Add 1 Tbsp. Rice Cooking Wine. Add 2 Tbsp. Soy Sauce or to taste.
Add in chopped Green Onions and a dash of Black Pepper. Mix well and serve.
